Rules 
Each Team must have 2 to 4 members.
While registering, each team needs to choose one of the given tracks and furnish the basic details about their team. The team also needs to choose whether they want to participate in the hackathon or the ideathon.
In the Hackathon, the participants will have to develop a working product (or a prototype) or a service-based business model to solve the problem statement for their chosen track
