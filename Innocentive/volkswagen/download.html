Benefits Of Electric Vehicles

By AdminJune 15, 2019No Comments

Here at EML Electrical we offer electric vehicle charging points and electric vehicle charging point leasing. There are so many benefits to electric vehicle charging points for your business. There are benefits of electric vehicles too.

In this blog post we have put together just some of the many benefits of electric vehicles.

  • Electric vehicles are cheaper to run as they have much lower running costs that petrol or diesel vehicles. The electric needed to charge a car for the same journey as a petrol or diesel car is around a third of the cost of fuel!
  • You will find that electric vehicles have a lot less moving parts so they need relatively minimal servicing and have less moving parts that can break and be expensive to fix or replace.
  • If you are a frequent driver to, in and around London that you will save costs in green zones and on congestion charges. This can save you huge amounts of money, dependent on how often you visit London.
  • An electric vehicle is a greener option causing less pollution in your local area. An electric vehicle has zero exhaust emissions. This means you are helping to reduce harmful air pollution.
  • Recent findings show that electric vehicles are actually safer vehicles for the driver and the passenger. This is because they have a lower centre of gravity meaning the car is less likely to roll over in an accident.
  • Electric vehicles are kinder on your neighbours too as they are very quiet. This means less noise pollution. It will also mean less waking the neighbours when you leave home early or get back late.
